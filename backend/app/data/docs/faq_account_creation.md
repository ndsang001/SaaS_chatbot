# Account Creation

## How do I create an account?

To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. 
Enter your email address, create a password, and confirm your email.

You must use a valid email address because a verification link will be sent to your inbox.

## Why didn’t I receive a verification email?

If you did not receive a verification email:
- Check your spam or junk folder.
- Ensure you entered the correct email address.
- Wait a few minutes, as delivery may be delayed.

If the email still does not arrive, you can request a new verification link from the login page.

## Can I change my email address later?

Yes. You can update your email address from your account settings page. 
You will need to verify the new email before the change becomes active.
