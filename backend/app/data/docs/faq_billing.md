# Billing and Payments

## What payment methods are supported?

We support credit cards and debit cards through secure payment processing.

## When will I be charged?

You are charged at the beginning of each billing cycle.
Monthly plans are billed every 30 days.

## Can I get a refund?

Refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.
Please contact customer support within 14 days of your billing date.
