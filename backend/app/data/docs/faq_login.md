# Login and Authentication

## I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. 
Enter your registered email address, and we will send you a password reset link.

The reset link expires after 30 minutes for security reasons.

## Why is my account locked?

Your account may be temporarily locked after multiple failed login attempts.
Please wait 15 minutes before trying again or reset your password.

## Can I stay logged in?

Yes. When logging in, you can select the "Remember Me" option. 
This keeps you logged in on your device for up to 7 days.
